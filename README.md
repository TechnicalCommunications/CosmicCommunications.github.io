# Cosmic Communications

## About
A learning journey exploring European Pagan traditions, with focus on Astrology and Sacred Geometry. Beginning studies in ritualism, celestial cycles, and geometric patterns found in nature and ancient European spiritual practices.

## Features
- **Home**: Introduction to cosmic communications and spiritual traditions
- **About**: Learn about the journey and philosophy
- **Astrology**: Discover the cosmic dance of celestial bodies
- **Sacred Geometry**: Explore geometric patterns in nature and ancient practices
- **Civil Code**: Understanding ethical and moral principles
